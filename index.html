Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at The University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Xihui Liu. Before that, I got my bachelor's degree in the CS Department at Harbin Institute of Technology in 2023, supervised by Prof. Wangmeng Zuo.

My research interests include but not limited to: 1. 3D Generation; 2. 3D Representation and Perception; 3. Combination of 3D Perception and Robotics. Please feel free to reach out to me via email for discussion or any opportunities. :)
